Bybit's deep integration with Mantle Network creates a distribution channel that reaches 80 million users and an institutional desk with existing commodity trader relationships.

## The Bybit-Mantle Connection

Bybit and Mantle are structurally connected — Mantle is a joint venture between BitDAO (Bybit's treasury DAO) and Mantle Network. This means:

- Mantle projects receive preferred access to Bybit's institutional desk
- Bybit's commodity trading clients (who already trade physical coffee futures) are reachable through Mantle ecosystem partnerships
- MNTUSDT trading on Bybit creates organic Mantle ecosystem visibility

## AsiliChain's Bybit Opportunity

### Buyer Portal Distribution

The primary commodity traders who buy Uganda coffee — Sucafina, Olam, Kawacom, Louis Dreyfus — are already active in crypto-enabled commodity trading. Bybit's institutional desk has existing relationships with these traders.

**The pitch:** AsiliChain gives commodity traders a verified EUDR-compliant supplier network. Instead of managing DDS documentation internally, they receive a verified API for every shipment.

### Retail Narrative

Bybit's 80 million retail users are familiar with RWA narratives. Uganda coffee RWA is:
- Tangible and understandable (everyone drinks coffee)
- ESG-aligned (smallholder farmer income)
- EUDR-driven urgency (regulatory mandate, not speculation)
- Mantle-native (fits Bybit's Mantle promotion)

## What AsiliChain Does Not Want

AsiliChain does not want Bybit to be an exchange listing target for a speculative token. There is no native AsiliChain token. The Bybit relationship is about:
- Institutional buyer portal distribution
- Commodity trader introductions
- Mantle ecosystem positioning

not about retail speculation on protocol revenue.
