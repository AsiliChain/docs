# Ecosystem & Partners

AsiliChain is built within the Mantle Network ecosystem — the world's largest Ethereum ZK rollup by TVL — with strategic relationships across institutional finance, payment infrastructure, and regulatory frameworks.

## Why Mantle

AsiliChain is on Mantle because Mantle is the only chain where all four of the following are simultaneously true:

| Requirement | Why Mantle | Alternatives |
|------------|-----------|-------------|
| **1-hour finality** | OP Succinct/SP1 ZK rollup — completed September 2025 | Ethereum: 7-day optimistic; Polygon: similar |
| **Institutional infrastructure** | TaaS, QCDT, $200M EcoFund, Bybit integration | No comparable agricultural RWA programme elsewhere |
| **EU regulatory alignment** | QCDT is the world's first DFSA-approved tokenised money market fund on any chain | Critical for MFI and EU buyer trust |
| **Near-zero gas fees** | $0.002 proving cost per transaction | BatchToken operations would be economically unviable on Ethereum mainnet |

## Strategic Relationships

### Mantle EcoFund ($200M)
The primary Phase 1 and Phase 2 funding target. See [Mantle EcoFund](./mantle-ecofund) for full strategy.

### Bybit Institutional
80 million users. Bybit's commodity desk has existing relationships with the institutional coffee traders (Sucafina, Olam, Louis Dreyfus) who are AsiliChain's primary buyer portal targets. See [Bybit Strategy](./bybit-strategy).

### Kotani Pay (Tether-acquired, October 2025)
The exclusive farmer payout rail. USDC → UGX → MTN MoMo in under 60 seconds. Tether's acquisition strengthens Kotani Pay's capital position and longevity.

### TransFi
The institutional on-ramp for MFI capital. Bank wire → USDC → LendingVault. ISO27001 + SOC2 Type 2 certification makes it acceptable to regulated financial institutions.

### Hedera HCS
IBM, Google, FedEx, Boeing governance council. The consortium backing makes Hedera HCS records acceptable to EU regulators and institutional buyers without further validation.
