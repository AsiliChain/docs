# AsiliChain's Relationship with Ethereum

AsiliChain is an **Ethereum L2 application** — built on Mantle Network, using Ethereum's security model, standard Solidity, and the full Ethereum developer toolchain.

## Mantle Is Now a ZK Rollup

:::info ZK upgrade completed September 16, 2025
Mantle completed its transition from optimistic rollup to **ZK rollup** using OP Succinct (powered by Succinct's SP1 zkVM). OP Succinct is a type-1 zkEVM — full EVM equivalence. All AsiliChain contracts work without modification.

| Metric | Before (optimistic) | After (ZK) |
|--------|---------------------|-----------|
| Finality | 7 days | **1 hour** |
| Withdrawal time | 7 days | 6 hours |
| Proving cost | N/A | $0.002/tx |
| TVL | ~$2B | **>$2B (world's largest ZK rollup)** |
:::

## Why This Matters for AsiliChain

**1-hour finality is the difference between MFI participation and rejection.**  
A 7-day challenge period is unacceptable for regulated capital. MFI treasury managers and compliance teams require institutional-grade settlement speed. 1-hour finality meets that requirement.
