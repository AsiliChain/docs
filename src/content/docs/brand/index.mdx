## Brand Identity

AsiliChain's brand identity reflects the origin story: the earth of Uganda's coffee highlands, the gold of the harvest, the forest green of deforestation-free certification, and the deep navy of institutional trust.

## Logo Usage

The AsiliChain symbol — a gold origin dot with three orbital arcs in Forest Green, Earth Brown, and Deep Navy — represents the supply chain in motion and the meaning of *asili* ("origin" in Swahili).

- Light backgrounds: use the standard full-color symbol (gold/green/brown/navy)
- Dark backgrounds: use the white version of the symbol
- Minimum clear space: 16px on all sides
- Do not distort, recolor, or modify the logo
- The symbol is available as an SVG for crisp rendering at any size
