This chapter tracks the assumptions that still need field validation before scale rollout. It is meant to be a living risk and evidence ledger for product, policy, and market execution.

Validation focus areas:

- Credit performance under multiple harvest cycles and weather shocks
- Cooperative operational throughput at district scale
- Buyer adoption thresholds for on-chain evidence and automated settlement
- Regulator acceptance of digitally assembled DDS evidence packages
