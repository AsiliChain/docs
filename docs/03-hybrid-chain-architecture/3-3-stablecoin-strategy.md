The stablecoin choices are not arbitrary. Each is selected for a specific use case and audience. cUSD on Celo is chosen because Kotani Pay's conversion rails to MTN Mobile Money Uganda are live and production-tested today. USDC on Mantle is chosen as the institutional settlement currency because it is MiCA-compliant for EU buyer interactions, has the deepest institutional acceptance of any stablecoin in regulated finance, and is natively supported on Mantle. AUSD from Agora is noted as a future treasury option once Agora formally expands its institutional onboarding programme to East African financial institutions.

| Stablecoin | Chain | Used For | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| cUSD | Celo | Farmer loan disbursements -> MTN Mobile Money via Kotani Pay | Active from Phase 1 |
| USDC | Mantle | Institutional lending settlements, MFI liquidity pool, EUDR buyer payments | Active from Phase 2 |
| AUSD (Agora) | Mantle | Treasury reserve, future institutional option | Phase 4+ (pending Agora East Africa onboarding) |
