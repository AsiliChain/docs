title: 5.1 Bridge Readiness
sidebar_position: 1
---

A critical prerequisite for the hybrid architecture is confirmed: Chainlink CCIP is live on both Celo mainnet and Mantle mainnet. This is production infrastructure, not a roadmap item. The Celo-Mantle lane is operational and the CCIP directory confirms active cross-chain lanes and supported tokens including bridged USDC. No bridge needs to be built from scratch - the protocol integrates existing, audited infrastructure.
