title: 10.5 Template for Other African Markets
sidebar_position: 5
---

Uganda's framework becomes the template. Kenya's equivalent regulators are the Central Bank of Kenya (CBK) for payment systems, the Sacco Societies Regulatory Authority (SASRA) for cooperative lending, and the Kenya Coffee Directorate (KCD) for sector oversight. Rwanda's equivalents are the National Bank of Rwanda (BNR), the Rwanda Cooperative Agency (RCA), and the National Agriculture Export Development Board (NAEB). Each expansion market requires a country-specific legal opinion on the same three questions: technology provider versus lender classification, payment service provider requirements for the MoMo off-ramp, and sector authority partnership for cooperative registry access.
