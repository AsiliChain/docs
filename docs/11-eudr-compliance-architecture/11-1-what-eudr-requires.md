title: 11.1 What the EUDR Requires
sidebar_position: 1
---

Under Article 4 of EU Regulation 2023/1115, every shipment of coffee entering the EU requires a Due Diligence Statement containing GPS polygon coordinates for every plot of land in the supply chain, deforestation-free proof against a December 2020 baseline, proof of legal production under local law, and operator declarations. The GPS polygon requirement alone is structurally impossible with paper-based cooperative records.
