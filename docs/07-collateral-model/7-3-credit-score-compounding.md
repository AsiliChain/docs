title: 7.3 Credit Score Compounding
sidebar_position: 3
---

CreditScore.sol starts every farmer at 500. Every on-time repayment adds 50 points; every default subtracts 100. The score is public, immutable, and portable across cooperatives and lenders. Over two or three seasons, a farmer who participates faithfully can access materially higher LTVs and lower rates than a new entrant. The score becomes an asset in itself - a credit history that no intermediary can confiscate.
