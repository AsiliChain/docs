title: 2.3 Africa-Wide Market Opportunity
sidebar_position: 3
---

Uganda is the entry point. Ethiopia, Kenya, Rwanda, Tanzania, and Burundi face identical traceability deficits and EUDR exposure. AsiliChain's architecture is designed from the start to be replicated across cooperative structures and regulatory environments with country-specific customisation of the USSD flow, GPS boundary tool, and language layer.
